What's a CDN?

  • Content Delivery Network

  • Network of servers in the cloudy ether

  • Makes static content delivery faster

How?

  • CDNs are better configured for static content

  • Their servers are spread all over the world so they are physically closer to your users

  • Your content is automatically copied to those servers around the world: you don't have to think about it!

Who provides CDNs?

  • Amazon

  • MaxCDN

  • NetDNA

Who's best?

  • They're all pretty good. For this demo we'll use Amazon.

  • We need Origin Pull, a feature Amazon has.
